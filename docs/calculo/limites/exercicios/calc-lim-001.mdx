# CALC-LIM-001 — Limite fundamental

<Exercicio>

Calcule:

$$
\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x}{x}
$$

</Exercicio>

<Solucao>

Sabemos que:

$$
\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x}{x} = 1
$$

</Solucao>
